Password Protection & Online Safety

Introduction to Password Safety

Passwords are like the keys to your online world. Every account you have—email, social media, gaming platforms, school accounts—needs a password. Just like you wouldn’t leave your house unlocked, you shouldn’t leave your accounts unprotected.

  • Protect your personal info (photos, emails, messages, files)
  • Keep your online reputation safe
  • Prevent hackers from using your identity
Example: Imagine someone logging into your social media account and sending messages pretending to be you. This could hurt your friendships or even get you into trouble.
Activity: List all the accounts you use online and check if you’re using passwords for each one. Note if any are weak or repeated.
