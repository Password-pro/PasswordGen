Password Tips & Security Advice

Strong Password Guidelines

  • Use at least 12 characters including letters, numbers, and symbols.
  • Avoid using easily guessable information like birthdays or names.
  • Use different passwords for each account.
  • Consider using a password manager for convenience and security.

How to Keep Your Accounts Safe

  • Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) whenever possible.
  • Regularly update your passwords, especially after security breaches.
  • Be cautious of phishing emails and fake websites.
